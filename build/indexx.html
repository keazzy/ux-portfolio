Elevate Security.

The old way of doing security awareness isn't cutting it. We helped elevate security move away from the dark cyber landscape, and crafted a conversational brand that resonated with the user on a human level.

We evolved the original elevate logo icon rather than replacing it. We redrew the icon on a 100x100 grid, creating a more solid and structured mark to anchor the rebrand on.

To build out the visual language, we created a system of coloured shapes that were dissected from a circle. Gestural marks were then added to the shapes to soften the harsh edges, and make them more friendly.

The Elevate Brand is strategically positioned itself as genuine, optimistic and refreshing — 3 traits that are rare in the cybersecurity world.

We used a bold 2-tone colour palette, choppy line-work and fresh typography to create a look that would stand out against its competitors.

Then, we combined all the brand elements into a familiar and functional marketing site.

Illustrated characters were then introduced to assist in the educational component of elevate. The dragosaurus 'Ellie' changes colour, texture and mood based on the security level of the user.
